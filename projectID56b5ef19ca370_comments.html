This book, of a fictional voyage to an "inner" world, is an example of early science fiction. It uses the concept of a hollow Earth first explored in Niels Klim's journey under the ground by Ludvig Holberg (PG: 27884) and best known from Jules Verne (A Journey into the Interior of the Earth, 3748).

This book appeared under a pseudonym. The author is most likely John Cleves Symmes, Jr..
